About

I'm a web developer with an unconventional journey into the tech world. Originally studying Educational Science, I've always had an inherent fascination with technology and programming. This curiosity compelled me to steer my career towards this exciting domain.

Not just an average coder, I bring a unique blend of skills to the table. As an empowerment trainer for women and gender-diverse individuals, I strongly believe in harnessing our inherent abilities and resources to create a more fulfilling life. My experience has taught me to navigate challenges resiliently and to think outside the box, which reflects in my approach to coding and web development.

As a web developer, my goal is not just to write code but to solve problems. From tackling complex issues to deciphering intricate puzzles, I see technology as a tool to find answers to big questions and effectuate social change. By mastering coding, I aspire to contribute to a more diverse and equal IT scene, breaking stereotypes and paving the way for a more inclusive tech community.
